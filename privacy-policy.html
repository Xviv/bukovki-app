Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Bukovki ("the App") is a children's educational app for learning to write Russian letters, developed by Daniil ("we", "us", or "our"). We take your family's privacy seriously — especially when it comes to children.

No Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Photos, videos, or audio
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Cookies or similar technologies

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases to unlock additional content (handwriting categories). All purchases are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not collect, store, or have access to any payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID data. Purchase history and transaction management are handled solely by Apple in accordance with their Privacy Policy.

No Internet Connection Required

The App works entirely offline, except when processing in-app purchases (which require a brief connection to Apple's servers). The App does not send any data to our servers or any third-party servers, and does not include any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising frameworks.

Local Data Only

All user progress and preferences are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device and is automatically deleted when the App is uninstalled.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

The App is designed for children aged 3–7. Because we do not collect any personal information whatsoever, we are fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

  • No personal information is collected from children or adults
  • No accounts or registration are required
  • No behavioral advertising or profiling
  • No social features or external links

Third-Party Services

The App uses Apple's StoreKit framework for in-app purchases. No other third-party services, SDKs, or APIs are used. The App does not contain any advertising.

Apple Privacy Nutrition Label

In accordance with Apple's App Store requirements, we declare: Data Not Collected. The App does not collect any data from users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
